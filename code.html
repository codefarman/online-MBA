What exactly does an online MBA program offer? expand_more

An online MBA offers the same rigorous curriculum as a traditional MBA, covering core business principles like finance, marketing, and management. It provides the flexibility to study from anywhere, making it ideal for working professionals. You'll gain advanced business knowledge, leadership skills, and a valuable network of peers, all in a convenient online format.

How much time is required to finish an online MBA? expand_more

Most online MBA programs can be completed in 18 to 24 months. However, many universities offer flexible pacing options, allowing you to accelerate your studies or extend them over a longer period to fit your personal and professional schedule.

Is the value of an online MBA comparable to a traditional MBA? expand_more

Yes. An online MBA from an accredited and reputable university holds the same value and recognition as its on-campus counterpart. Employers increasingly recognize the discipline and time management skills required to complete an online degree, making it a highly respected qualification in the corporate world.
